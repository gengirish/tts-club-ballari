Safety & Monsoon
Ties into the app's SOS
9:415G

Run Safe

🛑
Stop immediately if you feel…
Sharp or worsening joint pain · chest tightness or trouble breathing · dizziness or feeling faint · pain that builds as you run. When in doubt, stop — there's always another session.
🌧️
Monsoon advisory · Jun–Aug
Run 6–7 AM before heat and rain build. Treadmill fully counts on heavy-rain days. Avoid mossy tiles and waterlogged paths — sprains are the #1 monsoon injury. Arrive pre-hydrated.
Solo-run safety · Tue & Thu
  • Share live location with a trusted contact for the whole run
  • Keep one earbud out — stay aware on shared paths
  • Stick to familiar, well-lit, populated routes
  • Tell family your route and expected return time
  • Carry your phone on you, not left at a bench
